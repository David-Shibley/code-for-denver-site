Strengthening the Denver Community Through Innovative Solutions

CodeAcross 2015 is a weekend of civic problem-solving events hosted by chapters of the Code for America network around the world.

CodeAcross Denver 2015: February 21st, 2015 is a day full of discovering and creating innovative solutions with developers, entrepreneurs, city officials, nonprofits, writers, and people from diverse backgrounds and skill sets who are just passionate about empowering our Denver community.

By the end of the day, youâ€™ll have been part of a team that has collaborated on the design of technology-based solutions to address community issues like safety and justice, food systems, and economic development.

From training sessions on new perspectives for approaching any obstacle, to unraveling specific community issues with experts, to an idea-a-thon leading to three projects for 2015, this day is a launching day for a new chapter of Denver.

Be Part of the Movement: Join local nonprofits, city officials, developers, designers, and coaches at Galvanize on February 21st!
