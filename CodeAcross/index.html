CodeAcross DENVER

February 21 2015

Strengthen your local community

Join local non-profits, city officals, developers, designers, and coaches.

Start the day with tutorials of Agile Lean techniques and Rapid Protoyping to build quick actionable solutions. Then hear from local non-profits and how they look to increase their impact on Denver's community. In the afternoon, self organize to find the non-profit that interests you the most and create a collaborative backlog to help the Code for America Denver Brigade to work and build from.

Agenda

  • 8:00 - 9:00am Check In & Breakfast
  • 9:00 - 9:15am Opening Remarks
  • 9:15 - 10:15am Lean Agile Techniques Overview
  • Break
  • 10:30 - 11:30am Introduction to Rapid Prototyping
  • 11:30 - 12:30pm Lunch
  • 12:30 - 1:00pm Problem statementsfrom Local Non-Profits
  • 1:00 - 4:00pm Breakout groups: Rapid protoyping and backlog creation for non-profits
  • 4:00 - 4:30pm Proposed solution demonstration for each non-profit
  • 4:30 - 6:30pm Networking Happy Hour

Location

Code Across Denver will take place at Galvanize Denver. There is 2hr metered street parking surrounding Galvanize.
